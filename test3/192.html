10.6 Sundry Debtors and Sundry Creditors
In the ledger there are many personal accounts, some of them may show
debit balances, some others may show credit balances. If all the names are to be
written in the trial balance it will be unduly long. Therefore, a list of names with
the debit balances is prepared. This list is known as
‘Sundry Debtors’
(Sundry
means ‘many’). Similarly, a list of names with the credit balances is prepared.
This list is known as
‘Sundry Creditors’
.
