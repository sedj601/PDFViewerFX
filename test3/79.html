5.3.2 Posting of Compound Journal Entries
Compound or Combined Journal Entry is one where more than one
transactions are recorded by passing only one journal entry instead of passing
several journal entries. Since every debit must have the corresponding equal
amount of credit, special care must be taken in posting the compound journal
entry, where there may be only one debit aspect but many corresponding credit
aspects of equal value or vise versa. The posting of such transactions is done
in the same way as already explained.
Illustration 3 :
Jan. 12, 2003, Cash sales Rs.10,000, Cash received from
Kannan Rs.5,000 and commission earned Rs.2,500.
