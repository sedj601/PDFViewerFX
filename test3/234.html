After calculating the gross profit or gross loss the next step is to prepare
the profit and loss account. To earn net profit a trader has to incur many expenses
apart from those spent for purchases and manufacturing of goods. If such expenses
are less than gross profit, the result will be net profit. When total of all these
expenses are more than gross profit the result will be net loss
12.3.1 Need:
The aim of profit and loss account is to ascertain the net profit earned or
net loss suffered during a particular period.
