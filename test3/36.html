4.1.3 Receipt
When a trader receives cash from a customer, he issues a receipt containing
the date, the amount and the name of the customer. The original copy is handed
over to the customer and the duplicate copy is kept for record. In the same way,
whenever we make payment, we obtain a receipt from the party to whom we
make payment.
