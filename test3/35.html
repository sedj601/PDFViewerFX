4.1.2 Invoice or Bill
When a trader sells goods on credit, he prepares a sale invoice. It contains
full details relating to the amount, the terms of payment and the name and
address of the seller and buyer. The original copy of the sale invoice is sent to
the purchaser and its duplicate copy is kept for making records in the books of
accounts.
Similarly, when a trader purchases goods on credit, he receives a credit
bill from the supplier of goods.
